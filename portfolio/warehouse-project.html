Solar Plant Warehouse

DRD ERGO Mine Solar PV Plant Support Facility

← Back to Projects

Project Overview

Constructed as part of the 60MW Tshedza Solar Farm project, this warehouse facility provides critical storage and maintenance space for solar plant equipment and components.

Project Details

Client: DRD ERGO Mining

Location: Tshedza Solar Farm, Gauteng

Value: R15 Million

Duration: 4 months (2023)

Technical Specifications

Size: 1,200m²

Materials: Face brick walls, steel roof

Features: 5-ton overhead crane, secured storage

Special: Lightning protection system

Construction Highlights

  • Reinforced concrete foundation with 450mm footings
  • Structural steel frame with portal design
  • Face brick exterior with thermal insulation
  • Industrial-grade epoxy flooring
  • HVLS (High Volume Low Speed) ceiling fans
  • Secure access control system

Project Challenges

High Wind Loads

The exposed location required special engineering for wind resistance. We implemented additional bracing and used wind-rated cladding attachments throughout the structure.

Precision Crane Installation

The 5-ton overhead crane required millimeter-level precision in supporting steelwork. We used laser-guided alignment during installation to ensure perfect track alignment.
