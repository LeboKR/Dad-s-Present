60MW DRD ERGO Mine Solar SPV Plant

Client: DRD ERGO Mining

Value: R2 Billion

Duration: 2022-2023

Project Overview

This project involved the installation of 133,000 solar panels and construction of supporting infrastructure including:

  • 22KV Bess sub-station with 43 batteries
  • 6.6KV Consumer substation
  • Admin building and warehouse
  • 600mm double barrel culvert
  • Way bridge construction

Key Responsibilities

  • Managed site operations for the 60MW solar farm
  • Oversaw quality control for all civil works
  • Coordinated between electrical and civil teams
